The Basic Terms

Learn Some Basic Termenology

Algorithim
Step-by-step rules for solving a problem or completing a task.
Machine Learning
Machines learning and making decisions without explicit programming instructions.
Artifical Intelligence
Machines simulating human intelligence to perform tasks and make predictions
Neural Network
Computational model inspired by the human brain, used for pattern recognition.
Internet of Things (IoT)
Network of connected physical objects that collect and exchange data.
Big Data
Vast & complex datasets requiring specialized analysis.
